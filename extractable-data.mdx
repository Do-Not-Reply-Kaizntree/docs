## Batch Data

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What batch information can I extract from Kaizntree?">
    You can export a wide range of batch-level data, including (but not limited to):

    - Batch Code
    - Item Name / SKU
    - COGS (Cost of Goods Sold)
    - Available & Allocated Quantity
    - Unit of Measurement
    - Status
    - Supplier
    - Current Total & Average Cost
    - Location
    - Issue Date & Expiration Date
    - Country of Origin
    - Initial Stock Quantity & Cost
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Sales Order Data

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What sales order data can I extract?">
    Sales order exports include key metrics like (but not limited to):

    - Order Reference
    - Order Date & Paid At
    - SKU & Item Quantity
    - Price, Net Discount, Net Price
    - Tax
    - Landed Cost
    - Order Status
    - Financial & Fulfillment Status
    - Total Revenue, Fulfilled Revenue, Unearned Revenue
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
