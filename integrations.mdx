        **What does the Shopify integration do?**

        Kaizntree acts as a **two-way data bridge** with Shopify. It automatically pulls in all your essential sales and product data while pushing key updates back to Shopify, eliminating the need for manual entry.

        <AccordionGroup>
          <Accordion title="Integration Documentation">
            **Overview**

            Kaizntree acts as a two-way data bridge with Shopify, handling both data extraction and (pull) and data submission (push). It simplifies your life and reduces to 0 manual data entry as all essential info is passed along in one way or the other. Some info is not passed from Kaizntree to Shopify to maintain your data privacy (such as costs).

            **Data Pulling**

            Kaizntree is configured to automatically retrieve key data from Shopify. This includes:

            - Sales Orders:
              - Item sold
                - Name
                - Quantity
                - Price
                - Discounts
              - Total Discount
              - Customer
              - Date of Creation
              - Date of Shipping
              - Shipping Address
            - At the moment of integration:
              - At the moment of integration, Kaizntree also pulls in additional data to enable an even faster setup (no manual data input required):
                - Items
                  - All info
                - Stock (optional)
                  - All info
                - Item Statuses
                  - You can choose what status(es) to import (Active / Archived / Draft)
          </Accordion>

      <Accordion title="Amazon">
**What does the Amazon integration do?**

Kaizntree pulls **sales order data** directly from Amazon into your account, so you can track sales, manage stock, and fulfill orders—all in one place.
            - **Pulled from Amazon**:
              - Items sold (SKU, name, quantity, price)
              - Discounts, customer information, order/shipping dates, shipping address
              - Stock levels
            - Inventory can also be managed in Kaizntree based on Amazon sales.
            - The setup is fast and fully guided—just follow the steps in the integration flow to get connected.

      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="Etsy">
        **What does the Etsy integration do?**

Kaizntree acts as a **two-way data bridge** with Etsy. It automatically pulls in all your essential sales and product data while pushing key updates back to Etsy, eliminating the need for manual entry.

        - **Pulled from Etsy**:
            - Sales orders (items sold, quantity, price, discounts, customer, order/shipping dates, shipping address)
          - At time of integration: all item data, optional stock data, item statuses (Active / Archived / Draft)
        - **Pushed to Etsy**:
          - Inventory levels, product updates (excluding cost data for privacy reasons), sales order updates.

The setup is fast and fully guided—just follow the steps in the integration flow to get connected.
      </Accordion>

      <Accordion title = "QuickBooks">
        **Overview**

Our system acts as a two-way data bridge with QuickBooks Online, handling both data extraction (pull) and data submission (push). It simplifies the process of keeping critical business information synchronized, allowing you to focus on your core operations without worrying about manual data updates.

**Data Pulling**

The system is configured to automatically retrieve key data from QuickBooks Online. This includes:
        - Vendors: The system pulls vendor information, ensuring that your supplier records on Kaizntree are always current.
        - ⁠ ⁠Customers: Customer data like Name, Address, Phone No, Email is imported into the customer table on Kaizntree.
        - ⁠ ⁠Products: Products are imported, including information like SKU, Name and Cost about each item.
        - ⁠ ⁠Purchase Orders (Optional): If required, the system can also pull purchase orders to give you insights into your procurement activities.

**Data Pushing**
To ensure that your QuickBooks Online records reflect the latest business activities, our system supports data submission (push) for the following:
        - Sales Receipts: Sales receipts can be pushed to QuickBooks Online via the Sales Dashboard, recording sales transactions.
        - ⁠Purchase Orders: The system can push purchase orders, to QuickBooks Online from the Purchase Order Dashboard.
        </Accordion>

      **What does the Extensiv integration do?**

Kaizntree connects to **Extensiv** (formerly known as 3PL Central) to help you manage warehouse operations with real-time data sync.

    - Syncs **inventory levels, batch information, and fulfillment status** between your warehouse and Kaizntree
    - Keeps your system up to date as stock moves, orders are fulfilled, or items are received

This integration ensures your stock levels and fulfillment operations stay accurate across platforms.

    - Reduces misalignment between physical inventory and digital records
    - Helps manage multi-location operations or 3PLs with ease

Setup is quick and handled in collaboration with our team.
