        <AccordionGroup>
          <Accordion title="Integration Documentation">
            **Overview**

            Kaizntree acts as a two-way data bridge with Shopify, handling both data extraction and (pull) and data submission (push). It simplifies your life and reduces to 0 manual data entry as all essential info is passed along in one way or the other. Some info is not passed from Kaizntree to Shopify to maintain your data privacy (such as costs).

            **Data Pulling**

            Kaizntree is configured to automatically retrieve key data from Shopify. This includes:

            - Sales Orders:
              - Item sold
                - Name
                - Quantity
                - Price
                - Discounts
              - Total Discount
              - Customer
              - Date of Creation
              - Date of Shipping
              - Shipping Address
            - At the moment of integration:
              - At the moment of integration, Kaizntree also pulls in additional data to enable an even faster setup (no manual data input required):
                - Items
                  - All info
                - Stock (optional)
                  - All info
                - Item Statuses
                  - You can choose what status(es) to import (Active / Archived / Draft)
          </Accordion>

      <Accordion title="Amazon">
**What does the Amazon integration do?**

Kaizntree pulls **sales order data** directly from Amazon into your account, so you can track sales, manage stock, and fulfill orders—all in one place.
            - **Pulled from Amazon**:
              - Items sold (SKU, name, quantity, price)
              - Discounts, customer information, order/shipping dates, shipping address
              - Stock levels
            - Inventory can also be managed in Kaizntree based on Amazon sales.
            - The setup is fast and fully guided—just follow the steps in the integration flow to get connected.

      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="Etsy">
        **What does the Etsy integration do?**

Kaizntree acts as a **two-way data bridge** with Etsy. It automatically pulls in all your essential sales and product data while pushing key updates back to Etsy, eliminating the need for manual entry.

        - **Pulled from Etsy**:
            - Sales orders (items sold, quantity, price, discounts, customer, order/shipping dates, shipping address)
          - At time of integration: all item data, optional stock data, item statuses (Active / Archived / Draft)
        - **Pushed to Etsy**:
          - Inventory levels, product updates (excluding cost data for privacy reasons), sales order updates.

The setup is fast and fully guided—just follow the steps in the integration flow to get connected.
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="WooCommerce">
        **What does the WooCommerce integration do?**

Kaizntree acts as a **two-way data bridge** with WooCommerce. It automatically pulls in all your essential sales and product data while pushing key updates back to WooCommerce, eliminating the need for manual entry.

  - **Pulled from WooCommerce**:
      - Sales orders (items sold, quantity, price, discounts, customer, order/shipping dates, shipping address, all statuses)
      - At time of integration: all item data, optional stock data, item statuses (Active / Archived / Draft)
  - **Pushed to WooCommerce**:
      - Inventory levels, product updates (excluding cost data for privacy reasons), sales order updates/statuses.

The setup is fast and fully guided—just follow the steps in the integration flow to get connected.
