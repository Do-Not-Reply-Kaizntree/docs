<Steps>
<Step>
For uploading data related to items, batches of stock, and BOMs, simply click on the blue cross at the top-right of the page and on “Import Data”.
</Step>
<Step>
You will then be proposed different templates. Choose the one corresponding to what you want to upload, follow the template example, and upload the CSV file into Kaizntree.
</Step>
<Step>
Your data will be analyzed and, if there are no errors found, simply click on “Import” for your data to be auto-populated into your account. For data sheets under 1000 lines this process should take only a few seconds.
</Step>
</Steps>
