## What reports does Kaizntree offer?

Kaizntree provides a wide range of actionable reports, grouped into four main areas:

## Inventory Management
<AccordionGroup>
      <Accordion title="Top Selling Products Report">
        Best-performing SKUs by sales
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="Days Until Stock Out Report">
        Forecasts how long stock will last per SKU
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="Stock Quantity Over Time">
        Tracks inventory changes over time
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="Future Incoming Stock Report">
        Overview of incoming stock and expected arrival dates
      </Accordion>
    </AccordionGroup>
    
## Workflow Optimization
  <AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Materials Requirement Report">
      Lists what materials are needed to fulfill sales orders
    </Accordion>
    <Accordion title="Most Used Materials Report">
      Highlights the most frequently used materials in production
    </Accordion>
  </AccordionGroup>
    
## Sales and Demand Insights
<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Sales Over Time Report">
      Tracks sales trends over a defined period
    </Accordion>
    <Accordion title="Revenue by Sales Channel Report">
      Compares revenue across different sales channels
    </Accordion>
    <Accordion title="Top Customers Report">
      Ranks your customers by total sales, order frequency, or profitability
    </Accordion>
    <Accordion title="Sales Order Fulfillment Analysis">
      Includes data on net cost, net price, unearned revenue, and fulfillment status
    </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Financial & Profitability
<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Total COGS Report">
      Tracks cost of goods sold over a time period
    </Accordion>
    <Accordion title="Profit Per Item Report">
      Shows margins for each SKU
    </Accordion>
    <Accordion title="Average Cost Per Unit Report">
      Useful for forecasting and inventory valuation
    </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
